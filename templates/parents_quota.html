Reservation & Quota Guidance

Understand the different quotas and reservation benefits for your child’s admission process.

Why Reservations?

Reservation policies in India ensure equal opportunities for students from different backgrounds. These help promote social justice and provide access to higher education for underrepresented groups.

SC / ST Quota

Special reservation for Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes to uplift disadvantaged communities.

OBC Quota

Reserved seats for Other Backward Classes (non-creamy layer) to ensure fair opportunities.

EWS Quota

10% reservation for Economically Weaker Sections (General category families with income ≤ ₹8L).

PwD Quota

Reservation for Persons with Disabilities to support differently abled students.

Defence / Ex-Servicemen Quota

Special seats reserved for children of defense personnel and ex-servicemen.

State / Domicile Quota

Reserved seats for students belonging to a particular state or domicile region.

Documents Required

  • Caste / Category Certificate (SC/ST/OBC/EWS)
  • Income Certificate (for OBC/EWS)
  • Domicile Certificate (for State quota)
  • Disability Certificate (for PwD quota)
  • Service Certificate (for Defence quota)
